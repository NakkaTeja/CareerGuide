ITI

State and central governments have set up Industrial Training Institutes (ITI) to impart training in various technical skills from different engineering and non-engineering sectors. Since these courses help students secure jobs or become self-employed, they are known as vocational courses. ITI courses are open to students of classes eight, 10 and 10+2. The duration of the course can be one or two years. ITI also offers apprenticeships to help students gain practical experience in their skills.
